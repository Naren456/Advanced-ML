1.  **Q:** What is the "Cold Start" problem?
    *   **A:** The inability to recommend items to a **new user** (no history) or recommend a **new item** (no interactions).
    *   **Fix:** Use Content-Based filtering (use meta-data like genre/artist) or recommend popular items until history is built.
2.  **Q:** A user listens exclusively to 'Heavy Metal'. Your content-based system only recommends 'Heavy Metal'. What is this problem called?
    *   **A:** The **"Filter Bubble"** or Lack of Serendipity. The user is never exposed to new genres they might like.
